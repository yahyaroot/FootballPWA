This website is one website that provides some information about the French 1st league, Ligue 1 also called Ligue 1 Uber Eats due to sponsorship by Uber Eats, is a French professional league for men's association football clubs. At the top of the French football league system, it is the country's primary football competition. Administrated by the Ligue de Football Professionnel, Ligue 1 is contested by 20 clubs and operates on a system of promotion and relegation from and to Ligue 2.
